a cat had a home in a little house by a quiet street
he was small and orange with soft fur and big bright eyes
the house was his world and he knew every part of it
every morning he sat by the window and saw the world outside
he was always hungry and would meow until the girl gave him food
he was most happy when the girl gave him fish to eat
when night came he would curl up on the bed and sleep
he felt safe and warm inside and had no need to go outside
but one day the door was left open and the cat saw the garden
he crept to the door and saw the grass and the big tree in the yard
