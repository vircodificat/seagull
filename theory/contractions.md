# Contractions
In general, contractions are created by folding in a short suffix.
We include `*` as well to remind us of the apostrophe.

## Personal Pronouns
"To be" contractions.
Eg, "am", "are", "is".
Also includes "has".

* [I'm](*EUPL)
* [you're](*UR)
* [he's](H*ES)
* [she's](SH*ES)
* [it's](*TS)
* [we're](W*ER)
* [they're](TH*ER)

"have" contractions.

* [I've](*EUF)
* [you've](*UF)
* [we've](W*EF)
* [they've](TH*EF)

"would" and "had" contractions.

* [I'd](*EUD)
* [you'd](*UD)
* [he'd](H*ED)
* [she'd](SH*ED)
* [it'd](*TD)
* [we'd](W*ED)
* [they'd](TH*ED)
