Words that end in "-en" should be given a suffix of `/*N`.
[chosen](ChOhZ/*N)
[darken](DARCk/*N)
[kitchen](KICh/*N)
[weaken](WEaCk/*N)
[written](WRIT/*N)
[citizen](SIT/SE*N)
[drunken](DRU*NG/*N)
[driven](DRI*F/*N)
[given](GI*F/*N)
[heaven](HE*F/*N)
[proven](PRO*F/*N)
[seven](SE*F/*N)

When needed, split out a second consonant to the next stroke.
[foremen](FOR/MEN)
[regimen](REJ/MEN)
[specimen](SPES/MEN)
