# Basic Verbs

to be

* [be](B)
* [been](B-N)
* [being](B*G)
* [am](AM)
* [are](R)
* [is](S)
* [were](WR)
* [was](W-S)

.. note::

  "to be" verbs generally have their vowels dropped.
  Being one of those most common verbs in English, most are encoded with 1 or 2 key briefs.

to have

* [have](V)
* [had](H-D)
* [having](V*G)
* [has](H-S)

.. note::

  "to have" verbs generally have their vowels dropped.
  "have" is a bit weird in that it is an infold of `HV`.

to do

* [do](DO)
* [did](DID)
* [done](DOhN) - "done" has an infolded `E` to remind us that it has an "e" in it (unlike [don](DON)).
* [does](DOhS)
* [doing](DO*G) (to disambiguate with "dog")

helping verbs

* [would](W-D)
* [could](K-D)
* [should](SH-D)
* [can](K)


* [must](MU*S)
